Draft Legal Notice

This Privacy Policy is provided as a draft template. It must be reviewed and finalized by qualified legal counsel before publication. The wording below is conservative and intentionally avoids guarantees, but is not a substitute for legal review specific to Blueprint Biologics.

Information We May Collect

Through this website, Blueprint Biologics may collect:

  • Contact information, such as name, email, phone number.
  • Organization information, such as company name, organization type, website, state, and country.
  • Inquiry details, such as product interest, catalog requests, documentation requests, and wholesale application details.
  • Form context fields, such as source URL, submitted page, timestamp, product context, inquiry context, and account context.
  • Basic technical information that hosting providers may collect, such as IP address, browser, device, and security logs.

How We May Use Information

Blueprint Biologics may use the information provided through this website to:

  • Review and respond to inquiries.
  • Review wholesale account applications.
  • Route catalog, pricing, and documentation requests.
  • Maintain website security and spam prevention.
  • Improve website functionality and form workflows.
  • Comply with applicable legal or regulatory obligations.

Information We Do Not Want Submitted

Do not submit patient information, protected health information, medical records, diagnosis details, treatment details, Social Security numbers, payment card data, or other sensitive personal information through website forms.

Form Submissions and Service Providers

Website form submissions may be processed by our hosting provider and email delivery provider for the purpose of receiving and routing inquiries. Submissions may include the form fields you complete along with limited context fields such as a timestamp and source URL. Information may be transmitted to the email address Blueprint Biologics uses for inquiry intake.

Sharing of Information

Blueprint Biologics may share information with:

  • Service providers that support hosting, form processing, email notification, security, and website operations.
  • Internal team members or authorized representatives reviewing inquiries.
  • Legal, regulatory, or compliance advisors where appropriate.

Blueprint Biologics does not intentionally sell personal information submitted through website forms.

California Privacy Notice

California privacy laws may provide certain residents with rights related to personal information, depending on whether the law applies to the business and the type of information involved. Potential rights may include:

  • Right to know or access personal information collected.
  • Right to delete personal information, subject to exceptions.
  • Right to correct inaccurate personal information, where applicable.
  • Right to opt out of sale or sharing, where applicable.
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights.

To submit a privacy-related request, contact Blueprint Biologics through the contact information listed on this website. The applicability of these rights, and the timing of responses, depends on the specific facts and applicable law and should be confirmed with counsel.

Cookies and Analytics

At this stage, this website does not intentionally use advertising cookies or third-party analytics scripts. If analytics, advertising pixels, or tracking technologies are added later, this Privacy Policy should be updated before launch.

Data Retention

Blueprint Biologics may retain inquiry and application information for as long as reasonably necessary to review requests, maintain business records, prevent abuse, and comply with applicable obligations.

Security

No website or electronic transmission is completely secure. Blueprint Biologics uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures appropriate for a small business website, but cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

This website is intended for business and professional users and is not directed to children.

Updates to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Material updates should be reviewed with counsel before publication.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact Blueprint Biologics through the contact pathways on this website. A confirmed mailing address and privacy contact should be added before launch.
