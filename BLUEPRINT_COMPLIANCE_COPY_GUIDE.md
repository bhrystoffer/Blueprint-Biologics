# Blueprint Biologics, Compliance Copy Guide

A practical writing guide for safe, conservative, USA and California
conscious copy across the entire site.

Date: 2026-05-17
Applies to: all phases, all pages, all marketing copy

This guide is not legal advice. Final copy should be reviewed by qualified
counsel before launch.

---

## 1. Core Principle

Blueprint Biologics supplies research-grade compounds to qualified buyers.
The site sells a supply service to businesses and research facilities. It
does not offer medical care, treatment, or products for human or animal
use.

Every page must read as a B2B research supply company, not a clinic and
not a direct-to-consumer wellness brand.

---

## 2. Approved and Safe Language

Use these freely.

Product and use framing:
- "For research use only"
- "For laboratory research purposes only"
- "Not for human or animal consumption"
- "Not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease"
- "Research compound"
- "Research-grade compound"
- "Reference material"

Buyer and account framing:
- "Available to qualified buyers"
- "Qualified wholesale buyers"
- "Licensed research facilities"
- "Wholesale inquiries"
- "Research supply"
- "Bulk and wholesale supply"
- "Account application"
- "Qualified buyer verification"

Quality and documentation framing:
- "Documentation available upon request"
- "Specification documentation available to qualified buyers"
- "Tested for purity"
- "Purity documentation"
- "Consistent, verified quality standards"
- "Quality documentation provided with qualifying orders"

Responsibility framing:
- "Buyer is responsible for compliance with applicable laws and
  regulations"
- "Buyers must ensure their intended use complies with all applicable
  local, state, and federal laws"
- "Sold for legitimate research purposes only"

Fulfillment framing:
- "Reliable, tracked fulfillment"
- "Streamlined wholesale ordering"
- "Order processing and shipping"

## 3. Risky Language to Avoid

Never use the following.

Medical outcome words:
- "cure", "cures", "heal", "heals", "healing"
- "treat", "treats", "treatment", "therapy", "therapeutic"
- "reverse", "reverses", "prevent", "prevents"
- "diagnose", "prescribe", "administer", "dose a patient"

Outcome and benefit claims:
- "weight loss", "fat loss", "slim down"
- "anti-aging" as a benefit claim
- "boosts", "enhances", "improves performance"
- "safe and effective"
- "clinically proven", "doctor recommended"
- Any "before and after" framing

Status and identity claims:
- "FDA approved", "FDA-approved medication", "pharmaceutical grade" when
  it implies drug status
- "medication", "drug", "patient", "prescription"
- "we compound", "we formulate for patients", "we provide care"
- "get started with treatment", "start your protocol"

Tone and shipping red flags:
- "discreet shipping", "discreet packaging"
- "no questions asked"
- Anything that implies personal or consumer use

Do not name a compound and then attach a human benefit to it. Compound
names may appear only as neutral catalog data.

## 4. Footer Disclaimer Recommendation

Use this full disclaimer in the site footer on every page.

> All products supplied by Blueprint Biologics are for laboratory and
> research use only. They are not for human or animal consumption. Nothing
> on this site is intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any
> disease. Products are not drugs, medical devices, or approved
> medications. Blueprint Biologics does not provide medical advice and
> does not prescribe, diagnose, compound, or administer any product.
> Products are sold only to qualified buyers for legitimate research
> purposes. The buyer is solely responsible for compliance with all
> applicable local, state, and federal laws and regulations.
> Copyright Blueprint Biologics. All rights reserved.

## 5. Product Page Disclaimer Recommendation

Show a short disclaimer on every catalog product card and a full
disclaimer on every product detail page.

Short, for catalog cards:

> For research use only. Not for human or animal consumption.

Full, for product detail pages:

> This product is supplied for laboratory research use only. It is not
> for human or animal consumption and is not a drug or approved
> medication. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any
> disease. Sold only to qualified buyers. The buyer is responsible for
> compliance with all applicable laws and regulations. Documentation is
> available to qualified buyers upon request.

## 6. Contact Form Checkbox Disclaimer

Require a checked acknowledgment before any inquiry or quote form can be
submitted. The box must not be pre-checked.

Checkbox label:

> I confirm that I am a qualified buyer inquiring for research or
> wholesale purposes. I understand that all products are for laboratory
> research use only, are not for human or animal consumption, and are not
> intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. I accept
> responsibility for compliance with all applicable laws and regulations.

Optional second checkbox for account applications:

> I confirm the information provided is accurate and that I am authorized
> to purchase research compounds on behalf of my organization.

## 7. Wholesale and Account Qualification Language

Use qualification language to reinforce the B2B, non-consumer position.

Approved phrasing:
- "Blueprint Biologics supplies qualified wholesale buyers and licensed
  research facilities."
- "Wholesale accounts are available to qualified buyers. Account
  applications are reviewed before approval."
- "We may request documentation to verify buyer eligibility."
- "Pricing and ordering details are shared with approved accounts."
- "Custom labeling is available for qualified wholesale buyers."

Avoid:
- "Anyone can order"
- "No account needed"
- Consumer-style "shop now" or "buy now" calls to action

Prefer "Request Wholesale Pricing", "Apply for an Account", and "Request a
Quote" over "Buy" or "Shop".

## 8. Suggested Safer Rewrites for Existing Copy

These rewrites fix risky wording from the current live site while keeping
the business meaning intact.

### Hero
Current intent: "Premium Research Peptides at Scale" plus supplying
"research-grade compounds to licensed facilities, medical professionals,
and qualified buyers".

Safer rewrite:
- Headline: "Research-Grade Peptides, Supplied at Scale"
- Subhead: "Wholesale Peptide and Biologics Distribution"
- Body: "Blueprint Biologics supplies research-grade compounds to
  qualified buyers and licensed research facilities, with competitive
  wholesale pricing and quality documentation on request."

### Catalog category list
Current: "growth hormone peptides, anti-aging compounds, healing peptides,
cognitive enhancers, and specialty stacks".

Safer rewrite:
- "growth research peptides"
- "longevity research compounds"
- "repair and recovery research compounds"
- "cognitive research compounds"
- "specialty research blends"

### GLP-1 mention
Current: highlights "GLP-1 agonists including Semaglutide, Tirzepatide,
and Retatrutide" as a feature.

Safer rewrite: list these as neutral catalog entries by name only, under a
category such as "metabolic research peptides". Do not attach any benefit,
outcome, or weight-related framing.

### Who We Are pillars
Current: "Research Grade", "Bulk Pricing", "Custom Labels", "Fast
Fulfillment", with "discreet shipping".

Safer rewrite:
- "Research Grade", compounds tested against defined purity standards
- "Wholesale Pricing", competitive per-vial pricing on 10-vial boxes
- "Custom Labeling", available for qualified wholesale buyers
- "Reliable Fulfillment", streamlined ordering with tracked shipping

### Contact section
Current: "We work with medical professionals, clinics, and licensed
research facilities."

Safer rewrite: "We supply qualified wholesale buyers and licensed research
facilities. Reach out to set up an account or request a quote."

### Custom label note
Current: "Add $0.25/vial for custom labels (includes finished product)."

Safer rewrite: "Custom labeling available for qualified wholesale buyers,
add $0.25 per vial."

### Disclaimer banner
Current: "FOR RESEARCH USE ONLY | Not for human consumption | Wholesale
accounts only | All prices USD per vial | Custom labels: add $0.25/vial".

Safer rewrite: "For research use only. Not for human or animal
consumption. Wholesale accounts only. All prices in USD per vial."
Move pricing operational notes into the catalog, keep the strip focused on
compliance.

## 9. Quick Pre-Publish Checklist

Before any page goes live, confirm:
- [ ] No treatment, disease, or cure language
- [ ] No weight loss or human or animal outcome claims
- [ ] No "FDA approved" or medication-status implications
- [ ] No clinic, patient, or "we provide care" language
- [ ] No "discreet shipping" or consumer-use signals
- [ ] Compound names appear as catalog data only, no benefit framing
- [ ] Research-use disclaimer present where required
- [ ] Footer disclaimer present on every page
- [ ] Forms have the acknowledgment checkbox, not pre-checked
- [ ] CTAs use "Request", "Apply", or "Quote", not "Buy" or "Shop"
- [ ] Buyer responsibility statement present where products are listed

Final copy should be reviewed by qualified legal counsel before launch.
