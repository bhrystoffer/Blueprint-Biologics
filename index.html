Common Questions

Common Questions About Blueprint Biologics

Short answers to frequent questions. See the Quality and Documentation page for the full FAQ.

What is Blueprint Biologics?

Blueprint Biologics is a wholesale supplier of peptide and biologics compounds for qualified buyers and licensed research facilities. Products are intended for qualified wholesale, clinical, and professional accounts, subject to applicable laws and account review.

Who can request wholesale access?

Research laboratories, licensed clinics, licensed pharmacies, qualified distributors, academic institutions, and other qualified accounts may request access. Submissions are reviewed before account-based catalog support is provided.

Who can purchase from Blueprint?

Products are available to qualified wholesale, clinical, and professional accounts, subject to account review and applicable laws. Blueprint does not sell to the general public and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, prescribing, administration, dosage, or reconstitution instructions.

How do buyers request documentation?

Use the Documentation Request route or submit a wholesale inquiry. Documentation availability may vary by product, supplier, lot, and buyer qualification status.
