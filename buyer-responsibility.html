Draft Legal Notice

This Buyer Responsibility and Use Disclaimer is provided as a draft template. It must be reviewed and finalized by qualified legal counsel before publication. The wording below is conservative and intentionally avoids guarantees, but is not a substitute for legal review specific to Blueprint Biologics.

Qualified Account Access

Products, pricing, and documentation shown on this website are available to qualified wholesale, clinical, and professional accounts, subject to account review and applicable laws. Browsing the catalog does not grant any right to purchase. Submission of an inquiry or application does not guarantee account approval.

No Medical Advice or Instructions

Blueprint Biologics does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, prescribing services, compounding services, administration instructions, dosage guidance, reconstitution instructions, or patient-care services. Information on this website is general business information for qualified accounts and is not a substitute for advice from qualified professionals.

No FDA Approval Representation

Unless explicitly supported by verified documentation and reviewed by counsel, nothing on this website should be interpreted as representing that any referenced product is FDA-approved, approved for human use, approved for animal use, or suitable for any specific use. References to compounds, categories, or names are intended as catalog data for professional inquiry purposes only.

Buyer Responsibility

Buyer is responsible for compliance with all applicable laws, regulations, institutional rules, professional obligations, labeling requirements, storage requirements, transfer restrictions, resale restrictions, and use limitations that apply to the buyer's organization and jurisdiction.

Account Review

Blueprint Biologics may review account requests, organization details, and intended account context before providing catalog, pricing, documentation, or wholesale coordination. Submission of an inquiry or application does not guarantee approval. Account access may be limited, suspended, or revoked at Blueprint's discretion.

Product Information

Catalog information is provided for general business inquiry purposes and may change without notice. Product names, categories, formats, documentation, pricing, and availability may vary by supplier, lot, and qualification status.

Contact for Documentation

Reference documentation may be available to qualified accounts upon request. Use the documentation request route to submit an inquiry.
