Catalog FAQs

Catalog Access FAQs

Short answers about catalog access. See Quality and Documentation for the full FAQ.

How is catalog pricing shown?

Each product lists 1 vial and 10 vial box pricing in USD for blank vials. Custom label add-on pricing applies per vial where available. Pricing and availability may change.

How do I request product availability?

Browse the catalog by category, then submit a wholesale inquiry. Approved accounts receive availability details and pricing information.

Can I request documentation?

Yes. Documentation is available to qualified buyers upon request. Availability may vary by product, supplier, lot, and buyer qualification status.

Why are products listed by research category?

Products are organized by research category to support professional review. All listings are reference materials for laboratory research use only.
