Common Questions

Contact Page FAQ

Short answers about contacting Blueprint Biologics. See the Quality and Documentation page for the full FAQ.

What can I request through the contact form?

You can request product pricing, catalog details, reference documentation, custom label availability, wholesale account review, or general professional inquiries. Submissions are reviewed for qualified-buyer eligibility.

Can I request product pricing?

Yes. The catalog shows 1 vial and 10 vial box pricing for each product, and you can request a quote or confirm current pricing through this form. Pricing and availability may change.

Can I request documentation?

Yes. Documentation may be available upon request. Availability may vary by product, supplier, lot, and buyer qualification status.

Does Blueprint Biologics provide medical advice?

No. Blueprint Biologics does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, dosage guidance, administration instructions, or patient-care services. Products are intended for laboratory research use only and are not for human or animal consumption.
