Frequently Asked

Compliance and Process Questions

Common questions about Blueprint Biologics products, qualified buyer access, and documentation.

Who can purchase from Blueprint?

No. All products referenced on this website are intended for qualified wholesale, clinical, and professional accounts, subject to applicable laws and account review. Access is limited to qualified wholesale, clinical, and professional accounts.

Who can request wholesale access?

Wholesale access is available to qualified buyers, including research laboratories, licensed facilities, qualified distributors, and academic institutions. Blueprint reviews inquiries before providing account-based catalog support.

Is pricing public?

No. Pricing is shared with approved wholesale accounts. Submit a wholesale inquiry to request pricing details for your organization.

Can I request product documentation?

Yes. Documentation is available to qualified buyers upon request. Availability may vary by product, supplier, lot, and buyer qualification status.

Does Blueprint provide medical advice?

No. Blueprint Biologics does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, prescribing, compounding, or administration of any product.

Are products FDA-approved medications?

No. Products are not represented as drugs, medical devices, or approved medications. They are catalog items available to qualified wholesale, clinical, and professional accounts, subject to applicable laws and account review.

How do I request catalog details?

Browse the catalog by category, then submit a wholesale inquiry. Approved accounts receive catalog details, available documentation, and pricing information.
