Eligibility

Who Can Request Access

The following buyer types may request wholesale access. Approval depends on organization type, intended research-use context, and applicable regulatory obligations. Submission of an application does not guarantee approval.

  • Research Laboratory

    Laboratories conducting research workflows that involve reference compounds.

  • Licensed Clinic

    Licensed facilities reviewing research-use materials within applicable obligations.

  • Licensed Pharmacy

    Pharmacies reviewing reference materials for qualified research-use applications.

  • Qualified Distributor

    Distributors supplying qualified research buyers within applicable obligations.

  • Academic Institution

    Academic and research institutions supporting qualified research projects.

  • Other Qualified Buyer

    Other buyers that may be reviewed for access depending on context.

Buyer eligibility and documentation access may depend on organization type, intended research-use context, and applicable regulatory obligations. Application submission does not guarantee approval.

Wholesale FAQs

Wholesale Access FAQs

Short answers about wholesale access. See Quality and Documentation for the full FAQ.

Does submitting an application guarantee approval?

No. Submission does not guarantee account approval. Blueprint reviews applications to confirm qualified buyer eligibility before providing account-based access.

Who can apply?

Research laboratories, licensed clinics, licensed pharmacies, qualified distributors, academic institutions, and other qualified buyers may request access. Approval depends on organization type and intended research-use context.

What happens after applying?

Blueprint reviews the application. Approved accounts receive catalog details, available documentation, and pricing information. Orders, custom label inquiries, and fulfillment are coordinated with approved wholesale accounts.
